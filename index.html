Empowering Papua Through
Education & Human Development

SAGU Foundation is a local NGO in Jayapura, Papua — Strengthening, Assisting, Generating, and Utilizing human resources through quality education, English language training, and community development.

Our Programs Learn More

Education

Contributing to the advancement of education in Papua through targeted programmes and initiatives.

Workforce Development

Specialised programmes focused on developing practical skills and building workforce readiness, opening pathways to employment and scholarship opportunities that contribute to long-term development in Papua.

Research

Programme implementation is systematically documented and developed into research outputs for evaluation and continuous improvement.

The Background

“SAGU” is an acronym for “Strengthening, Assisting, Generating, Utilizing”.

Sagu (sago, Metroxylon sp.) is the staple food of the Papuan people and holds deep cultural significance in Papuan society.

Sago symbolizes the “mama”, the one who gives birth to and nurtures the children of Papua.

Inspiring Stories

Sa Tra Pernah Melangkah Sendiri

Follow the inspiring journeys of Papuan vocational school graduates who received the 2020 Beasiswa Siswa Unggul Papua, a scholarship programme from the Government of Papua that supports native Papuan students to pursue further study.

This book tells the story of their passionate steps in pursuing their dreams and studies in Australia. 🌏🎓
