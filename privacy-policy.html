Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how SAGU Foundation collects, uses, and protects personal information when you access our website and programs.

Last updated: May 2026

1. Information We Collect

We may collect information you provide directly, including name, email address, phone number, and other details submitted through contact forms, registrations, or partnership inquiries.

2. How We Use Information

We use collected information to respond to inquiries, manage educational programs, improve website services, and communicate relevant updates about SAGU Foundation activities.

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell personal information. Data may be shared only with authorized staff, trusted service providers, or when required by law for compliance and legitimate operational purposes.

4. Data Security

We apply reasonable technical and organizational safeguards to protect personal data against unauthorized access, misuse, alteration, or disclosure.

5. Your Rights

You may request access, correction, or deletion of your personal data by contacting us. We will review each request in accordance with applicable regulations and operational obligations.

6. Contact

For privacy-related questions, please contact us at info@sagufoundation.org.
