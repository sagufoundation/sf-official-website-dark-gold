Community Development

Information and resources related to community-focused projects and initiatives. SAGU Foundation brings education, outreach, and development opportunities directly to schools and communities across Papua.

Program Overview

The Community Development program at SAGU Foundation is built on a belief that lasting change begins at the grassroots level. Our tutors and volunteers go beyond the classroom — visiting schools, communities, and villages across Papua to deliver education, mentoring, and outreach sessions tailored to local needs. Through four active sub-programs, we create meaningful impact for students, educators, and entire communities.

Program Highlights

  • Tutor-led community projects designed to address specific local needs in Papua
  • School visits delivering English sessions, study skills workshops, and career motivation talks
  • Community outreach to villages and remote areas with limited access to education
  • Scholarship Club offering mentoring and preparation for domestic and international scholarships
  • Collaborative program design co-created with community leaders and local educators
  • Documentation and research of project outcomes for continuous improvement
  • Long-term relationship building with schools, institutions, and community organisations
  • Essay writing, interview preparation, and scholarship application guidance for aspirants

Ideal Participants

  • Primary, secondary, and university students seeking academic and personal development
  • Villages, community groups, and local organisations in Papua needing education support
  • Teachers and school administrators looking to collaborate and enhance classroom learning
  • Students aspiring to domestic and international scholarships who need mentoring and preparation
  • Motivated volunteers and interns eager to make a difference in Papuan communities
