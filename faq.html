Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers about registration, programs, and collaboration opportunities at SAGU Foundation.

How can I join SAGU Foundation programs?

You can contact our team via the Contact page or WhatsApp number listed on this website to get current schedule, requirements, and registration steps.

Are programs available for all age groups?

Program availability depends on each class category and period. Our team will guide you to the most suitable program based on your profile.

Does SAGU Foundation open collaboration with institutions?

Yes. We welcome partnerships with schools, universities, communities, and organizations aligned with education and community development goals in Papua.

How can I access updates on events and activities?

Please visit our Blog, Events, and social media channels. We share announcements and highlights regularly across those platforms.

Still have questions?

Our team is ready to help with detailed information about classes, partnerships, and community programs.
