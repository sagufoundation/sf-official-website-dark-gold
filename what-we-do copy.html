Our Impact

What We Do

SAGU Foundation contributes to the advancement of education and community development in Papua through targeted programmes and initiatives focused on education, workforce development, research, and capacity building. Our work is designed to expand opportunities, strengthen local capacity, and support long-term development across communities.

Our Focus Areas

Education

SAGU Foundation contributes to the advancement of education in Papua through targeted programmes and initiatives that strengthen access, improve learning outcomes, and support pathways to further study.

Workforce Development

SAGU Foundation develops practical skills and workforce readiness through targeted programmes that support pathways to employment, scholarships, and further study.

Research

SAGU Foundation documents programme implementation and develops these into research that supports evaluation and continuous improvement.

English Language Development

English language education has been one of SAGU Foundation's core areas of work since its establishment. We offer General English courses across various proficiency levels, English for Young Learners (EYL), IELTS and TOEFL preparation, English for Specific Purposes (ESP), workshops, and customised training programmes for schools, institutions, government agencies, and other organisations.

In addition to course delivery, SAGU Foundation develops contextualised English curricula and learning materials tailored to the needs of educational institutions, organisations, and local communities. Our materials reflect local cultures, educational settings, professional contexts, and specific learning objectives, making English learning more relevant, engaging, and applicable to participants' everyday lives.
