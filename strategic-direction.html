Strategic Direction

Our Strategic Direction

Since its founding, SAGU Foundation has been committed to advancing education and community development in Papua. As we step into a new decade, we continue to build on this foundation with a strategic focus that aligns with the evolving needs of local communities and the global landscape.

PHASE 1

2015–2024
Laying the Groundwork

Vision

Empowering Papuan communities through education and capacity building.

Mission

Delivering inclusive educational programs, skills development, and research initiatives that support sustainable growth in Papua.

PHASE 2

2025–2035
A Decade of Advocacy

Vision

To become a leading advocate for education across Papua by uplifting the voices and addressing the needs of marginalised communities.

Mission

To promote the importance of investing in 21st-century skills within local communities through inclusive, grassroots-driven approaches that build long-term capacity and opportunity.
