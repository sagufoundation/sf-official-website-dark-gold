Our Impact

What We Do

English Language Development

SAGU Foundation provides various English courses, including General English classes, English courses for children, and preparation classes for IELTS and TOEFL tests.

We also regularly hold workshops on English preparation and IELTS/TOEFL test readiness to help students achieve their academic and professional goals.

Our Focus Areas

Education

We are primarily focused on supporting the development of education in Indonesia, especially in Papua. SAGU Foundation aims to be an agent of change in the region.

Human Resource Development

SAGU Foundation organizes specialized programs aimed at supporting and enhancing skills, abilities, and capacities across various fields of work.

Research

SAGU Foundation documents and records various program implementation processes, which are then developed into research for evaluation and improvement.
